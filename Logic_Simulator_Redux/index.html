Digital Logic Simulator:

How to use -
simply select what block you want from the selection on the bottom of the screen and click to place it on the screen
Then hit the "esc" key to stop whatever action you are doing
Then click and drag on the white "nodes" found on each block to draw a wire
While drawing a wire click on the screen to connect when touching another white "node"
While drawing a wire you can also click to bend the wire; Remeber to cancel drawing hit the "esc" key To clear the whole screen hit the "~" key
